= Job aborted due to Snapshot autodelete
:icons: font
:imagesdir: ../media/

[.lead]
* *Message*
+
`Job aborted due to Snapshot autodelete`

* *Cause*
+
The volume does not have enough space and has triggered the automatic deletion of snapshots.

* *Corrective action*
+
Free up space in the volume and retry the job.
