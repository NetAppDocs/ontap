After you enable ARP, your ARP might enter a transitional period depending on your environment and ONTAP version:

[cols="25,25,50",options="header"]
|===
| Volume type | ONTAP version | Behavior after enablement

.2+| NAS FlexGroup
| ONTAP 9.18.1 and later
| ARP/AI is active immediately with no learning period

//no cell due to span
| ONTAP 9.10.1 to 9.17.1
| ARP starts in learning mode for 30 days

.2+| NAS FlexVol
| ONTAP 9.16.1 and later
| ARP/AI is active immediately with no learning period

//no cell due to span
| ONTAP 9.10.1 to 9.15.1
| ARP starts in learning mode for 30 days

| SAN volumes
| ONTAP 9.17.1 and later
| ARP/AI enters evaluation period to establish baseline
|===
